Synthesized Movement Lab

Ongoing development of a collaborative system for dancers and electronic musicians to perform live together. Designed as a series of prompts and rules with dancer Kaylin Maggard and musician Kengchakaj Kengkarna, a prototype was filmed in February 2023 by Danny Garfield.


A longer iteration, adding dancer Colin Heininger, was performed at James Cohan Gallery's Art in Conversation series in May 2023.


One of the prompts was published in Games for a Rainy Day by TXTbooks for the 2024 New York Art Book Fair.
