A prototype of an outfit generator/randomizer, inspired by Cher's closet in Clueless. A lifelong dream of both myself and Jessie Char, in Summer of 2022 we cataloged every clothing item we owned, and then built a little software prototype. The great Avery Trufelman interviewed us about it for the Articles of Interest podcast. I'm keeping the closet offline for now, but if you want to talk about it or check out the code please reach out!
