A prototype of an outfit generator/randomizer, inspired by Cher's closet in Clueless.

A lifelong dream of both myself and Jessie Char, in the Summer of 2022 we cataloged every clothing item we owned, and then built these little prototypes. The great Avery Trufelman interviewed us about it for the Articles of Interest podcast.
